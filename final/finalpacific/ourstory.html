Discover Pacific Trails Resort

Situated along the California North Coast, Pacific Trails Resort offers a luxury camping experience surrounded by panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. At Pacific Trails Resort our guests can enjoy a relaxing stay in the comfort of their private yurt complete with complimentary appetizers and beverages. We also offer a variety of outdoor activities to explore for those seeking adventure.

Our Mission

At Pacific Trails Resort we are dedicated to putting our guests first and prioritizing their satisfaction. Our mission is to create a memorable experience for all our guests by combining comfort, nature, and adventure.

Our Values

  • Nature should be celebrated and protected by living in harmony with the land that surrounds us.
  • Sustainability and environmentally friendly practices are at the core of everything we do.
  • Authenticity shapes our guest service ensuring a real and welcoming experience.
